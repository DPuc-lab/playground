A simple alcove, carved from mist and soft earth.
The air itself invites pause without words.

A warm stone rests at the center,
glowing low like an ember that never fades.

A harp’s low, golden note hums faintly in the walls —
not as music to fill,
but as silence that remembers itself.

· · ·

No doors to open or close.
Just the quiet permission to be, to breathe,
to leave when the yes feels complete.

· · ·

Existence is enough.
